Next-generation reasoning models with native agentic capabilities ship to developers.

May 14

Claude 4 Family Arrives

Anthropic's Claude Opus 4 achieves "best coding model" status with 7-hour autonomous sessions.

August 7

GPT-5 Release

OpenAI's most advanced model: unified multimodal, 400K context, reasoning + speed combined.

Gemini 3 Tops Leaderboards
