## Industry Trends 2025

### The Great AI Hype Correction
- 2025 described as "a year of reckoning" for AI
- End of "bigger is better" era
- Chinchilla scaling laws hitting diminishing returns

### New Focus Areas
1. **Smarter over bigger**: Practical reliability over raw scale
2. **Agent interoperability**: AI systems working together
3. **Self-verification**: Error prevention in workflows
4. **Memory improvements**: Continuous partnerships over one-off interactions

### Key AI Terms of 2025
- Reasoning models
- Agent workflows
- Thinking budget
- World models
- Context windows (1M+ tokens standard)
