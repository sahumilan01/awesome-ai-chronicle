# Contributing to Awesome AI Chronicle

Thanks for helping keep this chronicle current! Here's how to contribute.

---

## Ways to Contribute

### 1. Open an Issue (Easiest)

Use our [issue template](.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/new-entry.md) to submit a new entry. A maintainer will add it to the appropriate category.

### 2. Submit a Pull Request

1. Fork the repository
2. Add your entry to the appropriate category file in `categories/`
3. Follow the entry format below
4. Submit a PR with a clear title

## Quality Guidelines

### Do Include
- Verified, significant developments
- Primary sources (official announcements, author tweets)
- Clear, factual descriptions

### Don't Include
- Rumors or unverified claims
- Personal opinions or hot takes
- Duplicate entries
- Promotional content without substance

---

## Source Requirements

Every entry must have at least one source link:
- **Preferred:** Official announcement, author's tweet, company blog
- **Acceptable:** Reputable news coverage, research paper links
- **Not accepted:** Screenshots without links, secondhand reports

## Category Selection

| Category | What Goes Here |
|----------|----------------|
| `models-research.md` | Model releases, papers, benchmarks, training techniques |
| `tools-frameworks.md` | Libraries, APIs, developer tools, infrastructure |
| `products-applications.md` | Consumer apps, enterprise products, use cases |
| `industry-business.md` | Funding, M&A, hiring, policy, market analysis |

If unsure, mention it in your PR/issue and we'll help categorize.

---

## Review Process

1. Submit issue or PR
2. Maintainers review within 48 hours (typically faster)
3. May request minor edits for consistency
4. Merged and you get contributor credit!
