üî• September 12, 2024

OpenAI o1 Preview Released

OpenAI releases o1-preview, a new reasoning model that thinks before answering. This marks a paradigm shift from pure pattern matching to deliberative reasoning.

OpenAI o1 Reasoning
The Full Story

OpenAI o1 represents a fundamental shift in how AI models approach problem-solving. Unlike previous models that generate responses token-by-token, o1 uses "chain of thought" reasoning‚Äîthinking through problems step-by-step before producing an answer.

The model spends more compute at inference time, taking seconds to minutes to reason through complex problems. This approach excels at math, coding, and scientific reasoning, achieving new benchmarks on competitive mathematics and PhD-level science questions.

Key Capabilities
  • Ranks in the 89th percentile on Codeforces competitions
  • Achieves 83% on AIME (American Invitational Mathematics Exam)
  • Surpasses human PhD experts on GPQA science benchmark
  • Demonstrates improved safety through deliberative alignment

ChatGPT Launched

OpenAI releases ChatGPT, bringing conversational AI to mainstream attention. It reached 100 million users in just two months, becoming the fastest-growing consumer application in history and sparking the generative AI revolution.

OpenAI ChatGPT Consumer
The Full Story

ChatGPT's launch on November 30, 2022 marked a pivotal moment in AI history. Built on GPT-3.5, it was fine-tuned using Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF) to be more helpful, harmless, and honest in conversations.

Within 5 days, ChatGPT had over 1 million users. By January 2023, it had 100 million monthly active users, making it the fastest-growing consumer application in history‚Äîsurpassing TikTok's record by a factor of 10.

Key Impacts
  • Triggered the "AI arms race" between Google, Microsoft, and Meta
  • Led to Microsoft's $10B investment in OpenAI
  • Sparked global conversations about AI safety and regulation
  • Created the foundation for the agentic AI paradigm
